Functional traits

A functional trait is a morphological, biochemical, physiological, structural, phenological or behavioral characteristic that influences performance or fitness.Grouping species by functional characteristics is a long-standing idea, but there has more recently been rapid development in the aplication of trait-based approaches to diverse topic in ecology.\n This is an extract from the article Functional Traits (Charles A Nock et al., 2016)
